A Guide to Cardistry

Cardistry is the “performance art of card flourishing” where a deck of playing cards is manipulated with cuts, pivots, spins, twirls and other creative moves that create stunning displays and visuals. This website will teach you all about it, and even show you where to start if you are interested! To start all you really need is a basic set of 52 cards, and access to the internet or a friend who can help you out. If you get good, you will be able to impress virtually anybody, flex your skills, and have a great hobby and an amazing community.
